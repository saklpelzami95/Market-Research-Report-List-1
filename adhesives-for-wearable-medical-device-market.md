<p><p>The adhesives for wearable medical devices are categorized into acrylic-based, silicone-based, and other types. Acrylic-based adhesives offer strong bonding and good moisture resistance, making them suitable for prolonged skin contact. Silicone-based adhesives are known for their flexibility, hypoallergenic properties, and gentle removal, ideal for sensitive skin applications. Other adhesives may include polyurethane, rubber, or hot-melt types, each catering to specific needs in terms of biocompatibility, durability, and skin comfort, enhancing the performance of wearable medical devices.</p></p>
