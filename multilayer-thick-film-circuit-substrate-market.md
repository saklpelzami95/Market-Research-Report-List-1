<p><p>The multilayer thick film circuit substrate market primarily features two types: alumina substrates and aluminum nitride (AlN) substrates. Alumina substrates are widely used due to their excellent electrical insulation properties and mechanical strength, ideal for various electronic devices. In contrast, AlN substrates offer superior thermal conductivity, making them suitable for high-power applications where heat dissipation is critical. Both substrate types cater to industries like automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics, addressing different performance requirements and operational environments.</p></p>
