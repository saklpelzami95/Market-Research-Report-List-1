<p><p>The compact switchgear market is categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: high voltage, medium voltage, and low voltage switchgear. High voltage switchgear, typically used in transmission systems, operates at voltages above 36 kV, ensuring reliable power distribution. Medium voltage switchgear, ranging from 1 kV to 36 kV, is commonly utilized in industrial and utility applications for effective power management. Low voltage switchgear, operating below 1 kV, is essential for residential and commercial electrical systems, enabling safe and efficient power distribution.</p></p>
