Direct-light floor lamps focus illumination on specific areas, ideal for tasks like reading or working. They often incorporate adjustable brightness and color temperature. In contrast, Upward-light floor lamps direct light towards the ceiling, creating ambient lighting that diffuses warmth throughout the room. This type enhances the overall mood and atmosphere. Both types leverage smart technology for customizable settings, enhancing user experience and energy efficiency.</p></p>
