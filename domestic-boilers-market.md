<p><p>The domestic boilers market is mainly categorized into condensing and non-condensing boilers. Condensing boilers are highly efficient, capturing and reusing heat from exhaust gases, leading to lower energy costs and emissions. They are increasingly favored in modern installations. In contrast, non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency levels as they do not utilize exhaust heat, making them less environmentally friendly. The choice between these types often depends on factors like cost, installation requirements, and energy efficiency preferences.</p></p>
