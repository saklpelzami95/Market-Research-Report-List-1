<p><p>The thermal-curable optically-clear adhesives market is divided into two main types: single-component and two-component adhesives. Single-component optically-clear adhesives are pre-formulated for ease of use, requiring only heat to cure, making them ideal for applications needing quick activation. In contrast, two-component optically-clear adhesives consist of a resin and hardener that must be mixed before application, allowing for tailored performance characteristics and extended pot life. Both types are widely used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and optical devices.</p></p>
