<p><p>The multitasking CNC lathe market is divided into two primary types: horizontal multi-task CNC lathes and vertical multi-task CNC lathes. Horizontal multi-task lathes operate on a horizontal axis, efficiently handling complex machining tasks with improved accessibility and chip removal. In contrast, vertical multi-task lathes are oriented vertically, which allows for better rigidity and versatility in operations like milling, drilling, and turning. Both types enhance productivity, reduce production time, and improve precision in machining various materials.</p></p>
