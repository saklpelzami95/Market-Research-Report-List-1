<p><strong>The Speciality Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Rotary</li><li>Diaphragm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty pumps market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Centrifugal pumps employ rotational kinetic energy to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates. Reciprocating pumps utilize a back-and-forth motion for precise fluid delivery, suitable for applications requiring high pressure. Rotary pumps use the rotation of gears or vanes to transport fluids, ideal for viscous liquids. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create suction and pressure, excelling in handling corrosive or shear-sensitive fluids. Each type serves unique industrial needs.</p></p>
