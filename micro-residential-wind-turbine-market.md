<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Residential Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Residential Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro residential wind turbine market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal axis residential wind turbines and vertical axis residential wind turbines. Horizontal axis turbines, resembling traditional windmills, feature blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, offering higher efficiency and greater energy output. In contrast, vertical axis turbines have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, providing advantages in urban settings due to their compact design and ability to capture wind from any direction. Both options cater to homeowners seeking sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
