<p><p>The seed processing machinery market includes various equipment types essential for preparing seeds for sowing. Dryers reduce moisture content to ensure seed longevity, and coaters apply protective treatments for better growth. Separators classify seeds based on size or weight, enhancing quality. Polishers improve seed appearance and vitality by refining their surface. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving high-quality, viable seeds for agricultural use.</p></p>
