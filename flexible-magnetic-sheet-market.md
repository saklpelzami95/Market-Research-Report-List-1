<p><p>The flexible magnetic sheet market is divided into two primary types: self-adhesive and no stickiness. Self-adhesive sheets come with a sticky backing, allowing easy attachment to various surfaces, making them ideal for crafting, signage, and DIY projects. In contrast, no stickiness sheets are purely magnetic without adhesive, permitting users to position and reposition them on magnetic surfaces without leaving residue. Both types serve diverse applications across industries like retail, education, and home decor, offering flexibility and convenience.</p></p>
