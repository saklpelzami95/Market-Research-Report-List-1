<p><ul><li>UV Online Systems</li><li>VIS Online Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dissolution online systems encompass two primary market types: UV online systems and VIS online systems. UV online systems utilize ultraviolet light for real-time monitoring of the dissolution process, providing insights into drug release profiles and enhancing quality control. In contrast, VIS online systems employ visible light spectroscopy to assess the dissolution behavior, offering detailed analysis of solubility and concentration changes. Both systems aim to optimize pharmaceutical formulations, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and improving efficiency in drug development.</p></p>
