<p><p>Composite materials are engineered materials made from two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties. These materials combine the best characteristics of each component, resulting in enhanced performance across various applications. They are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, construction, and consumer goods due to their lightweight, high strength, and durability.</p><p>The Composite Material Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period. The expanding use of composites in renewable energy applications, particularly wind energy and solar panels, further fuels market expansion.
