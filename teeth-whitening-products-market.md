<p><strong>The Teeth Whitening Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Whiteners</li><li>Bleaches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The teeth whitening products market primarily includes surface whiteners and bleaches. Surface whiteners typically contain mild abrasives or polishing agents that remove surface stains caused by food and beverages, providing an immediate brightening effect. In contrast, bleaches contain hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide, which penetrate the enamel to lighten teeth from within, effectively addressing deeper discoloration. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from quick aesthetic improvements to more profound, long-lasting whitening solutions.</p></p>
