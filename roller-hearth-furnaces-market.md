<p><p>Roller hearth furnaces are critical in various heat treatment applications, categorized mainly into continuous and semi-continuous types. Continuous heat treatment furnaces allow for uninterrupted processing of materials on a moving roller system, optimizing efficiency for high-volume production. In contrast, semi-continuous heat treatment furnaces combine continuous flow with periodic loading, accommodating varying batch sizes while providing flexibility for different products. Both types enhance thermal uniformity and energy efficiency, catering to diverse industrial applications such as automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
