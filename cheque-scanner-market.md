<p><ul><li>Single-Feed Check Scanners</li><li>Multi-Feed Check Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cheque scanner market comprises two main types: single-feed check scanners and multi-feed check scanners. Single-feed check scanners are designed for processing one cheque at a time, making them ideal for low-volume transactions, providing simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, multi-feed check scanners can handle multiple cheques simultaneously, catering to organizations with higher transaction volumes. These scanners enhance operational efficiency, reduce processing time, and are widely utilized in banking, retail, and financial services to streamline cheque processing workflows.</p></p>
