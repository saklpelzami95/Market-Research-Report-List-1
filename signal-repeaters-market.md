<p><p>Signal repeaters are essential devices used to enhance and extend communication signals. The market consists of two primary types: analog and digital. Analog signal repeaters boost continuous signals without converting them into digital format, making them suitable for traditional communication systems. In contrast, digital signal repeaters process and regenerate discrete signals, ensuring better quality and performance in modern networks. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, broadcasting, and data transmission, catering to different technological needs and applications.</p></p>
