<p><p>The industrial polarization cameras market is segmented into two main types based on the polarization state of light: fully polarized light and partially polarized light. Fully polarized light cameras capture images where the light waves oscillate in a single direction, enhancing contrast and detail in monitoring industrial processes. In contrast, partially polarized light cameras capture images with a mix of polarized and non-polarized light, providing versatility in detecting reflections and glare, making them suitable for a broader range of applications in quality control and inspection.</p></p>
