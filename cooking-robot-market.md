<p><p>The cooking robot market comprises various types based on their design and functionality. Cartesian robots use linear movements along the X, Y, and Z axes, ideal for simple tasks. SCARA robots excel in assembly and ingredient placement due to their articulated arms. 6-axis robots offer flexibility and precision for intricate cooking tasks. Cylindrical robots utilize a circular motion, suitable for mixing and stirring. Delta robots are known for high-speed operations with multi-tasking abilities. Other types include specialized robots tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
