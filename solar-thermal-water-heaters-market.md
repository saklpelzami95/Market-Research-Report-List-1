<p><strong>The Solar Thermal Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure-bearing Solar Water Heater</li><li>Non-pressure Solar Water Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar thermal water heater market is primarily divided into two types: pressure-bearing and non-pressure water heaters. Pressure-bearing solar water heaters are designed to handle high-pressure systems, making them suitable for residences with pressurized water supplies, ensuring efficient hot water delivery. Conversely, non-pressure solar water heaters operate without pressure, relying on gravity for water flow. These systems are simpler and more cost-effective, ideal for locations with low water pressure or where a simpler installation is preferred. Both types capitalize on solar energy for efficient water heating.</p></p>
