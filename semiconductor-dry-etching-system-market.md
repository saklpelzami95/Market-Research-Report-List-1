<p><strong>The Semiconductor Dry Etching System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Reaction</li><li>Physical Removal</li><li>Combination of Chemical Reaction & Physical Removal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor dry etching system market comprises three primary types: chemical reaction, physical removal, and a combination of both. Chemical reaction etching utilizes reactive gases to selectively remove material from the substrate surface, achieving fine control over patterning. Physical removal relies on physical processes, such as ion bombardment, to displace material. The combination method integrates both techniques, enhancing precision and efficiency in etching processes. These approaches cater to diverse applications within the semiconductor manufacturing industry, optimizing performance and yield.</p></p>
