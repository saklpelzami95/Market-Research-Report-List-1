<p><p>The Tire Pressure Inflation Systems market is primarily categorized into Central Tire Inflation Systems (CTIS) and Continuous Tire Inflation Systems (CTIS). Central Tire Inflation Systems enable on-the-fly adjustments of tire pressure for optimal performance and traction, particularly in off-road vehicles. Continuous Tire Inflation Systems, on the other hand, maintain a constant tire pressure during vehicle operation, enhancing safety and fuel efficiency. Both systems are crucial for improving tire longevity, vehicle handling, and overall driving experience in various terrains.</p></p>
