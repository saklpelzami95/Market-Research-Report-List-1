Conditioning microphone preamplifiers improve signal quality by filtering noise. Differential microphone preamplifiers use two input signals to reduce interference. Electret microphone preamplifiers power electret microphones while amplifying signals. Far field microphone preamplifiers are tailored for distant sound capture. Fixed gain microphone preamplifiers provide constant amplification, while general-purpose models offer versatility for diverse applications across audio production, broadcasting, and telecommunications.</p></p>
