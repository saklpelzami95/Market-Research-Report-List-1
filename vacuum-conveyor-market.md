<p><p>The vacuum conveyor market is divided into integral and split types. Integral vacuum conveyors feature a unified design that combines the vacuum source and conveyor system into a single unit, ensuring compactness and ease of installation. In contrast, split-type vacuum conveyors consist of separate components, allowing for greater flexibility in configuration and easier maintenance. Both types serve industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, enabling efficient material handling while minimizing contamination and maximizing safety.</p></p>
