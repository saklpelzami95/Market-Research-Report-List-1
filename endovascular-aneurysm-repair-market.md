<p><p>Endovascular Aneurysm Repair (EVAR) is a minimally invasive surgical technique used to treat abdominal aortic aneurysms (AAAs) and other vascular aneurysms. Through small incisions, a stent graft is delivered to the site of the aneurysm via catheters, reinforcing the vessel wall and reducing the risk of rupture. This method offers several advantages, including shorter recovery times, reduced postoperative pain, and decreased risk of complications compared to traditional open surgical approaches.</p><p>The Endovascular Aneurysm Repair Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 8.9% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing prevalence of vascular diseases, advancements in technology, and the rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures. Furthermore, the aging population and enhanced awareness of vascular health are contributing factors to the market expansion. Key trends include the development of next-generation stent grafts with better compatibility and lower complication rates, as well as the incorporation of imaging technologies for improved procedural accuracy. Additionally, the growing emphasis on outpatient procedures is likely to further propel the demand for EVAR, making it a compelling segment in the overall medical device market.</p></p>
