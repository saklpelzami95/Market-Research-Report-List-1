<p><strong>The Neuroleptics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>First Generation</li><li>Second Generation</li><li>Third Generation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neuroleptics market is categorized into three generations based on their chemical composition and clinical use. First-generation neuroleptics, or typical antipsychotics, mainly target dopamine receptors and are effective for acute psychosis but have significant side effects. Second-generation neuroleptics, or atypical antipsychotics, address both dopamine and serotonin receptors, offering improved efficacy and reduced side effects. Third-generation neuroleptics aim for more balanced receptor targeting, focusing on partial agonism to minimize side effects while managing symptoms effectively, representing an evolving approach in psychiatric treatment.</p></p>
