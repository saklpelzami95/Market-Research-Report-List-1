<p><p>The insulin patch pump market is categorized into three main types: basal insulin, bolus insulin, and basal-bolus insulin. Basal insulin provides a continuous supply of insulin to manage blood glucose levels throughout the day. Bolus insulin delivers quick doses to control blood sugar spikes during meals. Basal-bolus insulin combines both types, allowing for a steady release of insulin alongside adjustable doses for meals. This flexibility supports personalized diabetes management, enhancing patient quality of life and adherence to treatment plans.</p></p>
