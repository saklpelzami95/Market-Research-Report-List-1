<p><p>The biconvex lenses market comprises two primary types: coated and uncoated biconvex lenses. Coated biconvex lenses have an additional optical coating to enhance light transmission, reduce glare, and minimize reflections, making them ideal for high-performance applications in photography, microscopy, and telecommunications. Uncoated biconvex lenses, on the other hand, lack this coating, resulting in lower production costs and simpler applications.
